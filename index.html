Snek Snack

Score: 0 High Score: 0

Game Speed:

Move your snake with arrow keys or WASD to eat the red apples. Be careful to avoid running into the walls or your own tail.

Blue obstacles will kill you if you run into them, but when "constricted" (surrounded on three sides by your snake) they will drop a golden apple that can be eaten for bonus points.
